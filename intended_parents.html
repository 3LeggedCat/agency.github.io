From the first conversation to the milestones ahead: how we care for you throughout your journey

Every step is guided by your priorities: we align on values, provide clear information, coordinate with your clinic, and keep communication steady, always with your path to parenthood in view.

Benefits offered to Intended Parents in the Novesse program:

  • Personalized donor matching aligned with your values, priorities, and preferred level of openness, with curated profiles and thoughtful guidance.
  • Comprehensive donor screening and readiness— medical, genetic, and psychological—coordinated with your clinic and in line with industry guidelines.
  • A dedicated care coordinator who manages timelines, answers questions, and provides proactive updates from match through retrieval.
  • Legal clarity and protections, including attorney referrals, contract facilitation, and transparent guidance on rights and responsibilities.
  • Transparent fees and secure financial management with itemized budgets, escrow administration, and no‑surprise billing.
  • Seamless clinic coordination and logistics— calendar planning, appointment scheduling, travel arrangements, and medication/monitoring coordination with your clinic.
  • Privacy, ethics, and ongoing support: confidentiality, inclusive care, counseling resources, and long‑term record‑keeping for future updates and sibling journeys.

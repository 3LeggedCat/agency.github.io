FAQ

Do I need previous experience?

No, you don’t need any experience. Our team will guide you through every step, making sure you feel informed and comfortable.

Does it affect my fertility?

No, the procedure only uses eggs that your body naturally discards each month, without impacting your ability to have children in the future.

What compensation is offered?

Compensation can be up to $35,000, depending on your profile and the family’s needs.

Can I donate if I use contraceptives?

Condoms and copper IUDs don’t affect the process. If you use oral contraceptives (the pill), you can also donate — we’ll just pause them during treatment. Hormonal IUDs or injections may be restrictive, so please consult with us.

How safe is the process?

All procedures are carried out under the strict supervision of specialized medical professionals, following the highest standards of safety and care.

How long does the process take?

It usually takes less than two weeks. During this time, you’ll attend short check-ups at the clinic, and the rest of the time you’re free to enjoy the city and your stay.
